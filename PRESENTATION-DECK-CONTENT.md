## 🎨 Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary**: Indigo (#4F46E5)
- **Secondary**: Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Accent**: Green (#10B981) for success metrics
- **Warning**: Yellow (#F59E0B) for alerts
- **Danger**: Red (#EF4444) for critical issues
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray (#F9FAFB)
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#1F2937)

### Fonts:
- **Headings**: Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat (Bold)
- **Body**: Inter, Open Sans, or Roboto (Regular)
- **Code**: Fira Code or JetBrains Mono

### Layout:
- **Consistent header** with logo and slide number
- **Large, readable text** (minimum 18pt for body)
- **High contrast** for readability
- **Plenty of white space**
- **Professional icons** (use Font Awesome or similar)
- **AWS branding** where appropriate

### Images:
- **Screenshots**: High-resolution, clean borders
- **Diagrams**: Simple, clear, color-coded
- **Icons**: Consistent style throughout
- **Charts**: Clear labels, easy to read
